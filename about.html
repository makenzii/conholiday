About Conventional Holiday

At Conventional Holiday, we pride ourselves on providing you articles and resources packed with valuable traditional insights about the traditions you celebrate and have come to love. One aspect of the holidays that many people long for is a connection with others and a connection to the past. one of the best way to achieve this is by sharing where our holidays came from and why we celebrate them in the fashion that we do. When this happens, we are able to find ourselves in the long line of generations and share that joy of discovery with those around us. Holidays are all about rekindling love and friendship and usually a time for remembrance. We want to bring you the conventions of Halloween, Thanksgiving, and Christmas to keep traditions alive and relationships strong.

Mankind is a great, an immense family. This is proved by what we feel in our hearts at Christmas."
Pope John XXIII
As we start this journey to catalogue conventional traditions of major holidays, we ask for your help in doing so. Please feel free to contact us on our Contact Us page to let us know what you want to hear about next from us. We want to know what stories, recipes, traditions, and customs you love or want to learn more about. We will do our best to keep adding to our resources and make them available to you here.
